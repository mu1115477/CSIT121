Supreme Omelet

Whip up a fluffly and satisfying omelet in minutes with this easy to follow recipe!

Ingredients:

  • 3 Eggs
  • Butter
  • 1 Green Pepper
  • 1 teaspoon Salt
  • 1 teaspoon Paprika

Instructions:

  1. Crack your three eggs into a bowl and mix them together.
  2. Chop up green peppers and add them to your egg mixture.
  3. Add a teaspoon of both paprika and salt to your mixture.
  4. Smear your small pan on medium heat with butter on it.
  5. Slowly pour your mixture in the pan.
  6. Watch your omelet carefully, as it begins to harden on one side, flip to the other.
  7. Once your omelet forms a firm consistency, place it on a plate and serve.

This is my favorite recipe because it takes 5 minutes to prepare and is filled with mouth watering flavor.
